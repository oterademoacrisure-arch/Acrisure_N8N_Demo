Insurance Policy Renewal Flow (Chat / Policy Upload)
1. Customer Initiation
- Customer starts via Chat or Policy Upload.
- Example (Chat): “I wish to renew my auto insurance policy. My Policy ID is POL‑2024‑001234.”
 
2. Customer Acknowledgement
- System sends acknowledgement to oterademoacrisure@gmail.com confirming receipt of the renewal request.
 
3. Quote Approvals (Carrier Side)
- Request for quote approval is sent to:
 
- AIG Sales force 
- progressivecgt@gmail.com (Approve? )
- travellerscgt@gmail.com  (Approve? )
- libertymutualcgt@gmail.com  (Approve? )
 
4. Customer Quote Selection
- Once quotes are approved, customer receives options via oterademoacrisure@gmail.com.
- Customer must select one of the three available quotes:
- Progressive
- Travellers
- Liberty Mutual
 
5. Finalization in Salesforce
- The selected quote, along with the policy ID and related details, is saved into Salesforce for record keeping and downstream processing.
